With the score accumulated in the passive phase, you will play gambles composed of the same images. In each trial, you will be presented with two of the images that you have learned about in the passive phase. By pressing the buttons in the scanner to move a cursor, you now have the option to choose to either: a) Accept gamble one, in which case you will be assigned one of one set of two images, or. . . b) Accept gamble two, in which case you will be assigned one of another set of two images.


The outcomes of your gambles will be hidden from you, and only 10 of them will be randomly chosen and applied to your current score.


Your final score will be recorded. Out of the top scorers, three will be given cash prizes as follows:

1st prize will be Rs 1500

2nd prize will be Rs 1000

3rd prize will be Rs 500
