1. For the passive phase, you will see a number in the middle of the screen.
(This is your current score for the day in)


2. When you see a NEXT button below the number, you are to press the NEXT button.


3. Shortly after pressing the NEXT button you will see an image on the screen background, and this will cause your score to change.


4. You are instructed to attend to any relationship between the images and the effect this has on your score.


Since in the active phase that follows you will be given the opportunity to choose images to influence your score. Learning theserelationships can make a large difference to your score in the active phase.
